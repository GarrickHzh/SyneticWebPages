About the Item

Brand New High Quality Extremely Bright 36 pieces High Power 3030 SMD LED lights per bulb 3157 shocket (aka 3057 3457 4157), 7443 shocket (7443, 7444NA, 7440, 7441, 992/7440A), and 1157 shocket (1016, 1034, 1157, 1157A, 1176) LED lights bulbs for turn signal and daytime running lights.

Features

  • Output per bulb: Amber: 380 lumen / White: 480 lumen
  • Type 1 Switchback
  • Socket Available: 3157 / 7443 / 1157
  • Application: Front Turn Signal Light (with dual filament)
  • Lifespan: 20000 working hours

Turn Signal Mode: Flashes back & forth in Amber Yellow LED & Xenon White LED Daytime Running Light: Turns on in Xenon White LED only.

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION::: When using LED bulbs for turn signal, you will need to use load resistor or decoder set to prevent hyper flash or bulb malfunction.
